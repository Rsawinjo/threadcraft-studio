About ThreadCraft Studio

Our story, mission, and commitment to excellence

Founded in 2018

ThreadCraft Studio was born from a passion for combining traditional craftsmanship with modern technology. What started as a small workshop has grown into a full-service custom apparel company serving businesses and individuals worldwide.

Our mission is simple: to make high-quality custom apparel accessible to everyone, while maintaining the highest standards of sustainability and craftsmanship. We believe that great design should never come at the expense of our planet.

Committed to Sustainability

Creating custom apparel that's kind to the planet

At ThreadCraft Studio, we believe great design doesn't have to come at the expense of the environment. We're committed to sustainable practices that reduce waste, conserve resources, and support ethical manufacturing.

  • Organic Materials: All our base garments are made from GOTS-certified organic cotton, free from harmful chemicals and pesticides.
  • Water-Based Inks: Our screen printing uses low-VOC, water-based inks that are safer for printers and the environment.
  • Recycled Packaging: We use 100% recyclable materials for shipping, and our facility is powered by renewable energy.
  • Ethical Sourcing: We partner with fair-trade suppliers to ensure workers are treated fairly and paid living wages.
  • Zero Waste Initiative: We minimize textile waste through precise cutting and donate scraps to local artisans.

Design Tips

  • Use high-resolution images (300 DPI minimum)
  • Vector files (SVG, AI, EPS) work best for embroidery
  • Simple designs with 2-3 colors print best
  • Avoid fine details under 1mm
  • Text should be at least 6pt font size
